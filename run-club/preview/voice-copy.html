Motivation / Runners' Bible

You're going to skip it.

Not this week, maybe — but at some point, you'll wake up at 6am and it'll be raining, or your legs will ache. And you'll decide that one week off won't hurt.

Here's what you lose: the moment at kilometer 4 where the sun comes fully over the ridge and someone next to you — a stranger three Saturdays ago — says something ridiculous and you both laugh while running.

That exact moment doesn't come back. It moves to next Saturday and happens to whoever shows up.
